The TSU stands for the Taylor Swift Universe and references artists that she's inspired and/or collaborated with. There have been interactions between Taylor Swift and each of these artists, but the most important part is that they all share the ability to write very personal songs about their own life experiences. This makes it very easy to apply these songs to our personal lives.
